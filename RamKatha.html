✦ About The Event ✦

Shri Ram Katha

A seven-day divine spiritual discourse that brings the eternal story of Lord Ram to life, inspiring millions with the timeless values of dharma, truth, and devotion.

Jai Shri Ram

Experience the Divine Narrative

Ram Katha is the sacred narration of the life and teachings of Lord Shri Ram, as described in the holy Ramcharitmanas composed by Goswami Tulsidas Ji. This seven-day spiritual event will take you on a transformative journey through the divine pastimes of Lord Ram.

Under the divine guidance of Pujya Sri Rajan Jee Maharaj, experience the transformative power of Ram Katha. This grand event is lovingly organized by Sailesh and his dedicated team to bring this sacred experience to the people of Bangalore.

Katha Vachak

Pujya Sri Rajan Jee Maharaj

Renowned Kathavachak & Spiritual Guide

Pujya Sri Rajan Jee Maharaj is one of the most revered Ram Katha narrators of our time. Maharaj Ji dedicated his life to the service of Lord Ram and the propagation of the sacred Ramcharitmanas.

Maharaj Ji's unique narration style blends profound scriptural knowledge with soul-stirring bhajans and kirtans, creating an atmosphere of divine ecstasy.

Having conducted hundreds of Ram Katha events across India and internationally, Pujya Rajan Jee Maharaj has inspired lakhs of devotees to walk the path of dharma, satya, prema, and bhakti.
